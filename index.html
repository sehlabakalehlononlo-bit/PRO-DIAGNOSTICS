About Us

Pro Diagnostics is built on a passion for automotive technology and precision. We deliver expert mobile fault-finding services that eliminate guesswork, reduce downtime, and put you back in control. Our technicians use cutting-edge diagnostic tools to identify engine, transmission, electrical, and electronic faults with unmatched accuracy.

From sporadic check engine lights to complex ECU issues — we provide on-the-spot, professional reports and transparent advice. Serving individuals, fleets, and dealerships, our mission is to redefine convenience in vehicle repair. With years of hands-on experience across all major brands, Pro Diagnostics ensures your vehicle receives the highest standard of care, right at your doorstep.
